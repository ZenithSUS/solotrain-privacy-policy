SoloTrain Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

SoloTrain ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the SoloTrain app.

1Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide and improve our app experience:

  • Personal Information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app usage analytics
  • Workout & Progress Data: Exercise history, statistics, achievements, and training preferences
We never sell your data to third parties. Your training journey stays yours.

2How We Use Your Information

Your data powers your fitness journey. We use it to:

  • Provide and maintain core app features and functionality
  • Track and visualize your workout progress over time
  • Improve app performance, fix bugs, and optimize user experience
  • Personalize your training recommendations and achievements
  • Send important updates about your account or the app (with your permission)

3Data Storage & Local Files

SoloTrain stores certain content locally on your device for offline access and better performance:

  • Workout videos and exercise demonstration images are cached in your device's storage
  • This allows you to train without an internet connection
  • You can clear cached files through your device settings at any time

These files are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

4Sharing & Third Parties

We maintain strict control over your information. We may share limited data only to:

  • Provide essential analytics and app performance insights
  • Comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of SoloTrain, our users, or others
No personally identifiable information is ever sold or shared for marketing purposes.

5Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission and secure storage practices
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data within our team

While we strive for maximum security, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information at any time
  • Clear locally cached workout files through your device settings
  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • Delete your account and associated data permanently

7Children's Privacy

SoloTrain is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

8Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through the app or via email
  • Provide a summary of key changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.

?Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or how we handle your information, we're here to help.
